Deliverable · architecture diagram

No new hardware.
Every input already exists.

VAYU fuses feeds India already pays for — CPCB stations, Sentinel/MODIS satellites, IMD forecasts, traffic feeds, municipal permit registries — onto a 1 km city grid, runs three AI engines on it, and pushes decisions out. The feedback loop retrains the models after every intervention.
